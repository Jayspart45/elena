AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. It refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans, and perform tasks that would typically require human intelligence. AI enables machines to analyze data, make decisions, solve problems, understand natural language, and even interact with humans. It encompasses various subfields such as machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics. AI has applications in various fields like healthcare, finance, transportation, and many others, and has the potential to greatly impact society and industries.
